Albert Einstein

“Any fool can know. The point is to understand.”

- Albert Einstein


Here's a timeline of Albert Einstein's life:


1879 - Born in Ulm in the Kingdom of Württemberg in the German Empire, into a family of secular Ashkenazi Jews

1896 - enrolled in the four-year mathematics and physics teaching diploma program at the Federal polytechnic school in Zurich

1900 - Einstein's paper "Folgerungen aus den Capillaritätserscheinungen" ("Conclusions from the Capillarity Phenomena") was published in the journal Annalen der Physik

1901 - Worked as an assistant examiner – level III at the Swiss Patent Office, in Bern

1903 - Married Mileva Marić

1905 - Einstein's annus mirabilis (amazing year), he published four groundbreaking papers, on the photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity, and the equivalence of mass and energy

1906 - Einstein was awarded his PhD

1908 - Appointed lecturer at University of Bern

1913 - Became a member of the Prussian Academy of Sciences in Berlin

1915 - Einstein had successfully completed his general theory of relativity

1916 - Einstein was elected president of the German Physical Society

1916 - Einstein predicted gravitational waves, ripples in the curvature of spacetime which propagate as waves traveling outward from the source transporting energy as gravitational radiation

1918 - Einstein was one of the founding members of the German Democratic Party, a liberal party

1920 - Became a Foreign Member of the Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences

1921 - Visited New York for the first time

1922 - Was awarded The Nobel Prize in Physics "for his services to Theoretical Physics, and especially for his discovery of the law of the photoelectric effect".

1926 - Einstein and his former student Leó Szilárd co-invented the Einstein refrigerator

1930 - Patented the Einstein refrigerator

1933 - Einstein resigned from the Prussian Academy

1935 - Einstein collaborated with Nathan Rosen to produce a model of a wormhole, often called Einstein–Rosen bridge

1940 - Officially became an American citizen

1955 - died at the age of 76
